[INT. Kitchen – Late Night]
The hallway is dark. The kitchen glows soft yellow under warm lights. Emi quietly tiptoes in, only to find Ingram already there, leaning against the counter with a glass of water.

EMI:
Oh—! Sorry, I didn’t think anyone was still up.

INGRAM (glances over, tired smile)
You have a habit of sneaking into quiet places.

EMI:
Says the guy who’s always already there.

(Beat. She shuffles over to grab a cup, leaning beside him. There’s a soft silence.)

EMI:
I couldn't sleep, so I found myself here.

INGRAM:
That makes two of us. I can still hear Kai's voice ringing in my head.

EMI: (chuckles)
He really is loud, huh?

EMI:
...It was really fun though. I didn’t think a silly game like that could make everyone open up so much.

INGRAM: (murmurs)
You’re surprisingly bold under pressure.

EMI: (blushing instantly)
Bold?! You didn’t even react when I—when Annelise dared me—!

INGRAM: (smirking, eyes on the mug)
You didn’t miss, if that’s what you’re worried about.

EMI:
Please forget about it!

INGRAM:
Oh, but it was such an unforgettable memory with everyone...

EMI:
You can erase that one moment from your head!

(She laughs again, but it softens. A lull follows.)

EMI:
Still... I wish times like that could last forever.

(Ingram grows quiet. She glances over. His gaze is lowered.)

EMI:
...Did I say something weird?

INGRAM: (shakes his head slowly)
No. You just reminded me... that they don’t.

(He’s not trying to ruin the moment—his voice is soft, even regretful. It’s clear he wishes it could last too.)

EMI:
...

INGRAM: (quietly)
This place isn’t meant to be forever. You’re all meant to move on.

EMI: (soft)
And you?

INGRAM: (glances at her)
I stay.

(Emi studies him. The atmosphere quiets, becoming heavier.)

EMI:
Ingram... earlier, during the game, I realized something. I don’t really know anything about you.

(He glances at her—neutral—but doesn’t deflect.)

INGRAM:
You asked me once... if I was human.

(She stiffens slightly. He sets the glass down.)

INGRAM:
I am. Or—I was. I don’t really know anymore. I’m a vagabond, same as the rest of you.

INGRAM:
Except... I don't remember anything from before. Only that me being here was a choice. I chose to stay. 

EMI: (whispers)
You... chose to stay?

INGRAM: (shakes head)
I made a deal. With the spirit within the Sweven Mirror. Kagami.

(His voice lowers on the name, as if it still holds weight.)

INGRAM:
In exchange for staying here... I gave up my memories. Whatever pain I wanted to escape—it must’ve been bad enough that I was willing to forget who I was.

INGRAM:
I was named "Ingram" when I became a spirit guide, but in truth, I don't even remember my original name.

(Pause. Emi looks at him, eyes wide, heart caught in her throat.)

EMI:
Do you ever wonder why you did it?

INGRAM:
I used to. All the time. Now, it's just a small voice at the back of my head.

INGRAM:
But I also wonder... if even now, I'd still make the same choice.
